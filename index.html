Welcome to our adoption centre

We rehome pets in Ireland. Please find information below about what to expect during the rehoming process.



Pet Adoption Process

  1. Our rehoming center is open on Friday, Saturday, Sunday and Monday from 1-3pm. Outside these hours, we operate on appointment-only basis for potential adopters.
  2. Make Plans - If you are hoping to welcome a pet into your life, please plan ahead and ensure that it will fit into your lifestyle long term.
  3. Pre-Adoption Talk - You can attend our pre-adopting talk online from the comfort and safety of your home. You'll get a chance to ask questions of our team members and find out more about how we can help your new pet settle in.
  4. First Date - Depending on your new pet's needs, we'll sometimes arrange for you to meet them before they come home and get to know them a little better. You may need to meet them multiple times before they join you home.
  5. Happy Life - Get advice and support from our team.
(text source: dogtrust.ie)
